fix(ai): auto-retry + fallback model on Gemini overload (503)

The 503 "UNAVAILABLE / high demand" error is a transient Google-side spike.
The SEO generator now retries transient failures (429/5xx/network) with
exponential backoff and, if the primary model stays overloaded, automatically
falls back to GEMINI_FALLBACK_MODEL (default gemini-2.5-flash). 404 model-not-
found also falls through to the fallback; 400/401/403 fail fast. Friendlier
"model is busy, try again" message on persistent overload.
